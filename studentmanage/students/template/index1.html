Accountant

An Accountant helps businesses make critical financial decisions by collecting, tracking, and correcting the company's finances. They are responsible for financial audits, reconciling bank statements, and ensuring financial records are accurate throughout the year.

Become a IT Engineer

An IT Technician is a professional who is responsible for installing, maintaining and repairing hardware & software components of the organization's computers. They also support these systems through remote access or site visits as needed by management teams within their company.
